What is TrustScore?

🛡️

Employer Credibility

TrustScore (0-1000) measures your company's credibility as an employer. Candidates see this when viewing your jobs.

✓

Verification Status

Verified companies with work email domains automatically get a verification badge and score boost.

📝

Job Authenticity

AI analyzes your job postings for completeness, realistic requirements, and clear salary information.

📊

Hiring Ratio

Your interview-to-offer ratio shows candidates you have fair, efficient hiring practices.

⭐

Candidate Feedback

After interviews, candidates can rate their experience. Positive feedback boosts your score.

🎯

Platform Behavior

Response times, profile completeness, and regular activity all contribute to your score.
